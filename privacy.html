Privacy Policy for Deep Pulse (Official English Version)

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 23, 2026

1. Introduction

Deep Pulse ("we," "our," or "us") provides a high-end digital wellness platform focused on synchronized breathing, visual neural-sync, and relaxation. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a secure user experience.

2. No Data Collection

Personal Information: Deep Pulse does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal identification data (PII).

Health & Biological Data: We do NOT access, collect, or upload any sensitive health data, heart rate information, or biological metrics to external servers.

Local Processing: All visual rendering, breathing rhythm algorithms, and audio synchronization are performed strictly on your device.

3. Data Security

Since no user data is collected or transmitted by the app, there is no risk of your personal data being compromised via our platform.

4. In-App Subscriptions

All financial transactions are processed securely through the Google Play Billing System. We do not have access to your credit card details or billing information.

5. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the developer information provided on the Google Play Store.
